Technology and Functionality

Liquid 3 functions through a bioreactor filled with microalgae, a plant-like organism that is highly efficient at photosynthesis. Microalgae absorb carbon dioxide (CO2) from the air and release oxygen (O2), similar to a tree but at potentially higher rates. The installation is powered by solar energy, ensuring that the system remains eco-friendly and sustainable.

Environmental Benefits

Liquid 3 offers significant environmental benefits, particularly for urban areas struggling with high levels of air pollution. The microalgae bioreactor helps reduce the concentration of CO2, contributing to the fight against climate change. By converting CO2 into oxygen, it actively improves the quality of air, making urban environments healthier for residents.

Educational and Awareness Role

Beyond its functional benefits, Liquid 3 serves as an educational tool to raise public awareness about innovative solutions for climate change and sustainability. The transparent design enables passersby to observe the process of photosynthesis in action, sparking curiosity and discussions about ecological advancements.
